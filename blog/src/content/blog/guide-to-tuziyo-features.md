Welcome to **Tuziyo**, a focused workspace for multi-model AI image generation. You can explore different generation models and keep prompts, references, settings, and results together in reusable sessions.

New accounts receive **10 free generation credits**, with no credit card required.

Here is a complete guide to what you can do with Tuziyo.

## 1. Generate with multiple AI image models

Open the [AI Studio](https://tuziyo.com/ai-toolkit), describe the image you want, add references when the selected model supports them, and choose the output controls that fit the brief. Tuziyo keeps the prompt, settings, and results together so you can compare iterations without rebuilding the workflow.

If you are unsure where to start, use the [AI image model comparison](https://tuziyo.com/ai/models) to compare current resolution, reference, credit, and use-case details.

## 2. Choose a model for the brief

The [model guide hub](https://tuziyo.com/ai/models) summarizes the options available inside Tuziyo. Review details such as supported resolutions, reference capacity, controls, starting credit cost, and recommended use cases before you generate.

Use those constraints to narrow the choice:

- Select a reference-capable model when visual consistency matters.
- Review supported resolutions before planning a final composition.
- Check the credit cost when you expect to iterate several times.
- Match the model's strengths to product imagery, graphic design, campaign visuals, or exploratory concepts.

## 3. Keep the creative trail in reusable sessions

Each generation session keeps the prompt, references, settings, and outputs together. Instead of rebuilding a brief after every attempt, return to the session, review earlier results, and continue from the strongest direction.

This is especially useful when you want to test prompt changes, revisit a reference set, or understand which settings produced a particular result.

## 4. Start with free generation credits

Every new account receives **10 free generation credits**, and no credit card is required. Credit usage depends on the selected model and settings, so check the displayed cost before starting a generation.
